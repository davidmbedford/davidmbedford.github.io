Bio

I’m a web developer with 8 years of experience in community organizing and a deep passion for coding technology.

Having worked on multiple grassroots campaigns at the state and university level, I have experience coordinating large teams, conducting press outreach, designing concise, attractive messaging, and overall balancing many tasks at once. I’ve served as a public speaker in classrooms and at public events to audiences ranging from 10 to 3,500 people. I’ve served as an arbiter for multiple organizations, and from this have learned much about conflict resolution and democratically incorporating the ideas of a whole team into one strong project.

I have dev experience with JavaScript (ES6), HTML5, CSS3, Node.js, SQL, MongoDB, and various JS and CSS libraries such as React and Materialize. As a musician drawn to the creative potential of code, my desire to learn is boundless.
