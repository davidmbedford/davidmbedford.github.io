Bio

I’m a web developer with 8 years of experience in community organizing and a deep passion for UI/UX design.

Having worked on multiple grassroots campaigns at the state and university level, I have experience coordinating large teams, conducting press outreach, designing concise, attractive messaging, and overall balancing many tasks at once. I’ve served as a public speaker in classrooms and at public events to audiences ranging from 10 to 3,500 people. I’ve served as an arbiter and lead coordinator for multiple organizations, and thus have learned much about project management, conflict resolution, and democratically incorporating the ideas of a whole team into one strong project.

I have dev-experience with JavaScript (ES6), React, jQuery, HTML5, CSS3, node.js, MySQL, and MongoDB. I can build dynamic web applications in a variety of ways (most recently with a focus on MERN stack, sometimes with MySQL in place of Mongo). And I use CSS libraries such as Materialize and Bootstrap to ensure cross-platform responsive-design.

As a musician, I have many years of experience with various digital audio workstations such as Ableton Live, Cubase, and Reaper. I'm also familiar with Adobe Photoshop and Premiere. I often find myself drawn to the creative aspects of programming, whether that come in the form of illustrative design, or dealing with new challenges under the hood. I'm always looking forward to learning something new, and inspired by the seemingly endless possibilities of code!
